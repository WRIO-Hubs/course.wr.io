Meet Your Personal Business Coach

With a solid technical background, I embarked on my entrepreneurial journey as a co-founder of an IT development company. Our team rapidly expanded to over 40 developers, and we created a platform for a client that grew from a valuation of $50 million to $1.2 billion, thanks to our implementation of automation. Later, I founded a separate company called wr.io and developed cutting-edge semantic technology that received two EU grants. This technology will be used to create advanced tools for streamlining micro-business processes on the BizCom platform, which my subscribers will have early access to soon.

My extensive experience in the corporate sector has driven my focus towards small companies. Through the years, I have spearheaded the growth of multiple SMEs and startups that have successfully secured funding from VCs and grants. However, after consulting with the first dozen, I realized that my approach needed to change, as I was spending up to six months on a single company. That's why I am developing my program for MSMEs, which aims to automate the creation of microenterprises, providing everything necessary for launching and sustaining businesses, significantly reducing the percentage of companies that fail.

At the heart of my conviction is the belief that ideas and knowledge are not competitive resources, but rather resources that benefit the more people use them. This belief is the foundation of the Project.

Furthermore, the growth of GDP and progress is directly correlated with the growth of the population. However, it's not just about the number of people, it's about the number of connections between them. The stronger the networking, the wider and faster the spread of ideas, and the more opportunities for partnership and earning potential.

With the emergence of new tools and the rapidly growing trend of AI, individuals and small teams can now implement ideas that were previously only available to companies.

This vision is the driving force behind my departure from the corporate sector and concentration on small businesses, which are highly flexible and quick to adopt innovations.

But my mission is not just to work with micro-enterprises around the world, but to unite them in a 1 million Big Empire of Micro Enterprises.

Why? Not for money itself. To achieve a more equitable distribution of resources and the emergence of a stable society, I see the development of tools that allow small businesses to compete with large corporations as crucial.

That's why I want to help people escape the 9-5 grind, become micro- and small business owners, and develop their own businesses capable of providing financial independence and fostering local communities. The more people who use ideas, the greater the positive effect on society. This is the foundation of my business.

My travels have given me a firsthand understanding of the urgent need to make an impact on developing countries.

As a result, I am currently assembling a team to help address these issues.

I want to assist entrepreneurs who are willing to earn a living for their families while also improving the lives of those around them.

I want to help people who believe that by improving the lives of others, they can improve their own lives.

For me, happiness should be the top priority, not just income.

In conclusion, my firsthand experiences while travelling have highlighted the urgent need to make a positive impact on developing countries. As a result, I am currently assembling a team to help address these issues. My goal is to assist entrepreneurs who are willing to earn a living for their families while also improving the lives of those around them because they believe that by improving the lives of others, they can improve their own lives.

If you share these values and want to join a growing community of like-minded individuals, sign up for my course today.
